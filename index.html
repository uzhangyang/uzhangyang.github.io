About

        I am a professor at Department of Computer, School of Information Science and Engineering, Hebei University of Science and Technology (HeBUST). Currently, I am leading intelligent software Lab. I received my doctoral degree from Beijing Institute of Technology. I was a visiting scholar at Department of Computer Science, Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Xiangyu Zhang (2017-2018). My research interests include intelligent software engineering, software evolution, parallel programming, and deep learning.

        E-mail: zhangyang@hebust.edu.cn
