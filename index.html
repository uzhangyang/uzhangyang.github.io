About

        I am an associate professor and the vice dean at Department of Computer, School of Information Science and Engineering, Hebei University of Science and Technology. I received my master and doctoral degree from Hebei University of Engineering and Beijing Institute of Technology, respectively. I was a visiting scholar at Department of Computer Science, Purdue University, working with Prof. Xiangyu Zhang from Jan, 2017 to Jan, 2018. My research interests include intelligent software, software refactoring, parallel programming, and deep learning.
