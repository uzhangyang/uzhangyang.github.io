About

        I am a professor at School of Information Science and Engineering, Hebei University of Science and Technology (HeBUST). Currently, I am leading intelligent software Lab. I received my doctoral degree from Beijing Institute of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Feng Shi and Prof. Weixing Ji. I was a visiting scholar at Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Xiangyu Zhang, and then at Federation University Australia working with Prof. Guojun Lu and Dr. Grant Meredith. My research spans intelligent software engineering, software evolution, parallel programming, and deep learning. I am passionate about software refactoring.
