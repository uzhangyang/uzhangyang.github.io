About

        I am an associate professor at Department of Computer, School of Information Science and Engineering , Hebei University of Science and Technology (HEBUST). Prior to joining HEBUST, I received my master and doctoral degree from Hebei University of Engineering and Beijing Institute of Technology, , respectively. I am a visiting scholar at Department of Computer Science, Purdue University, working with Prof. Xiangyu Zhang from Jan, 2017 to Jan, 2018. Currently, my research interests include parallel programming, software refactoring, concurrent bug, software ananlysis , and aspect-oriented programming.
